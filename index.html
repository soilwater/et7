ET7 — Weekly Irrigation Estimation Tool

Purpose. ET7 provides weekly estimates of reference and crop evapotranspiration (ET) to help guide irrigation scheduling decisions in Kansas. Values are practical approximations and are not a substitute for direct soil moisture measurements, commercial irrigation scheduling services, or site-specific agronomic advice.

Crop coefficients. Basal crop coefficients (Kcb) are approximations based on FAO-56 guidelines representing typical Kansas growing conditions. Actual crop water use will vary with variety, management, and local weather.

Limitations. Estimates assume typical crop development and do not account for unusual weather, pest or disease stress, soil variability, or irrigation system inefficiency. Always combine these estimates with field observations.

Development. Developed by the Soil Water Processes Lab, Department of Agronomy, Kansas State University. Climate data derived from GRIDMET (Abatzoglou, 2013) using the ASCE Penman-Monteith standardized equation for short reference crop ET.
